LLM Insurance Chatbot Evaluation

This report presents a comparative analysis of six large language models within the context of a question-answering chatbot designed for Insurance companies. The models underwent evaluation using a dataset derived from real customer conversations with human customer service representatives, providing a benchmark (golden answer) against which model outputs were compared. 

The major question it focused on answering is which is the best model for Insurance company use case. The report checks two initial aspects-the verbosity of models and their capabilities in handling sensitive data. Furthermore, the report explores performance-cost trade-offs, exploring the possibility of substituting high-cost OpenAI models with more economical open-source alternatives without significantly compromising performance.
